Welcoming Event Bain Dallas

Today was our welcoming event with the Dallas Office. I was super excited about it! I got to meet a lot of people. I made a special connection with someone in particular, a partner at the firm. Lamb of God in Spanish!

Last summer, I had a strong prompting to start translating Lamb of God to Spanish, the musical portrayal of Jesus' final week on earth. I did not understand the reason behind it, but I knew that it would benefit all Spanish speakers. Miracle after miracle, I found people to set it up in Spanish here in Utah. It is HAPENNING! I am so excited and grateful to share this with others. This is the flyer for audtions. Invite everyone to come!!
